Actionable Tips

Concise, practical actions you can take right away to reduce costs and make better mobility choices.

🔍 Shop insurance annually

Compare coverage and premiums; look for multi-policy and safe-driver discounts.

🧮 Use the cost calculator

Run the calculator to compare lifetime costs for different models and ownership options.

Open calculator
🗺️ Combine and plan trips

Batch errands and plan routes to reduce mileage and fuel use.

🔧 Check tyre pressure monthly

Proper pressure and alignment improve fuel economy and tyre life.

💳 Negotiate financing

Compare banks, credit unions and dealer offers; small rate differences add up over a loan term.

🔍 Pre-purchase inspection

For used cars, get a mechanic inspection and service history to avoid surprise repair costs later.

⚡ Consider EV incentives

Check local rebates, tax incentives and charging support that reduce EV costs.

🚗 Try car sharing or subscriptions

If you drive infrequently, these can be more cost-effective than full ownership.

📊 Track parking & operating costs

Monitor parking, fuel/charging and maintenance to spot quick savings opportunities.
