Alternatives

Compare typical annual costs and find a mode of transport that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Public transit

~ USD 1,200–2,400 / year

Season tickets reduce cost for regular commuters. In many Swiss cities, excellent network coverage.

Biking

~ USD 200–600 / year

Low marginal cost, health benefits, weather-dependent.

E-bike / Pedelec

~ USD 400–1,200 / year

Good for longer commutes with less sweat; higher upfront cost but low running costs and fast urban speeds.

Electric scooter / Micromobility

Pay-per-ride or ownership ~ USD 150–800 / year

Convenient for short urban hops; check local regulations and safety equipment.

Carpooling / Ride-pool

Shared costs — USD 300–1,500 / year

Share commute costs with colleagues; can lower parking and fuel expenses and reduce congestion.

Car sharing (round-trip / free-floating)

~ USD 600–3,000 / year

Pay-per-use for occasional drivers; avoids insurance, parking and depreciation but check hourly/km rates.

Subscription / Car-as-a-Service

USD 4,000+ / year (varies)

Includes insurance, maintenance and sometimes charging; higher cost but predictable and flexible.

Hybrid vehicles

Moderate purchase cost; lower fuel use

Good middle-ground for long-distance drivers who want better fuel economy without full EV infrastructure.

Telecommuting / Flexible work

Reduces commute-related costs substantially

Work-from-home or hybrid schedules can reduce miles driven and total ownership cost; negotiate with employers for partial remote days.

Park-and-ride / Multimodal

Transit + parking fees — variable

Drive to a suburban park-and-ride, then take transit into the city to save on inner-city parking and congestion charges.

Buy older / high-mileage used car

Lower purchase price; higher maintenance risk

Lower upfront cost but budget for maintenance and possible repairs. Good if you can DIY or access cheaper local mechanics.

EV subscription or short-term lease

Higher monthly cost; low commitment

Test EV ownership without long depreciation risk — subscription services bundle insurance and maintenance.
